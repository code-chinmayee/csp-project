Understanding Ayurveda

Ayurveda, meaning "Science of Life", is a 5,000-year-old Indian system of natural healing. It focuses on achieving balance among the body, mind, and spirit through diet, lifestyle, and herbal medicines.

Principles of Ayurveda:

  • Balance of the three doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha
  • Use of herbs, oils, and natural substances
  • Emphasis on detoxification and rejuvenation
  • Daily routine (Dinacharya) and seasonal regimen (Ritucharya)

Diseases Treated:

  • Digestive disorders (Acidity, IBS)
  • Skin problems (Psoriasis, Eczema)
  • Joint pains and arthritis
  • Stress and insomnia
  • Respiratory conditions like asthma, allergies

Common Ayurvedic Remedies:

  • Triphala: For digestion, detoxification
  • Ashwagandha: Reduces stress, increases strength
  • Turmeric: Anti-inflammatory, boosts immunity
  • Neem: Skin and blood purifier
  • Brahmi: Improves memory, calms the mind

Example Treatment Plan:

Problem: Acidity and Gas
Treatment: Take warm water with Ajwain (carom seeds) and Jeera (cumin) post meals. Use Triphala powder before sleep. Avoid fried/spicy food.

Problem: Joint Pain
Treatment: Use Mahanarayan Tailam oil massage, followed by hot water bath. Consume turmeric milk and Guggulu-based tablets under guidance.
