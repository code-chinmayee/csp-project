Understanding Yoga

Yoga is an ancient Indian discipline combining physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation. It promotes harmony between body, mind, and nature. Yoga is not only a physical exercise but a spiritual path to wellness.

Core Elements of Yoga:

  • Asanas (physical postures)
  • Pranayama (breath control)
  • Dhyana (meditation)
  • Yamas and Niyamas (ethical practices)

Diseases Treated with Yoga:

  • Back pain, neck pain
  • Obesity and diabetes
  • Hypertension and heart problems
  • Depression, anxiety, and stress
  • Asthma and breathing issues

Examples of Asanas and Benefits:

  • Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose): Good for back pain, digestion
  • Shavasana (Corpse Pose): Relieves stress and calms the mind
  • Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation): Full-body exercise for flexibility
  • Vrikshasana (Tree Pose): Improves balance and focus
  • Paschimottanasana: Helpful in managing diabetes and digestion

Sample Yoga Routine:

Problem: Stress and Anxiety
Routine: Practice Anulom-Vilom (alternate nostril breathing), Shavasana, and Meditation for 15–20 minutes daily. Avoid caffeine and maintain a calming evening routine.

Problem: Obesity
Routine: Surya Namaskar (10 rounds), Trikonasana, and Ardha Matsyendrasana daily along with Kapalbhati pranayama. Avoid oily and junk food.
